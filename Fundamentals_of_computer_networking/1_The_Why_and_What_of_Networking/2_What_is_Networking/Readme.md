*What is Network?*

1. Network means connecting multiple devices with each other. 
2. If minimum two devices are connected to each other then it is a Network. e.g. If your laptop is conneted to your mobile through hotspot then it is a Network. 
3. Other devices like your tablet, desktop, TV are also connected to your mobile thorugh hotspot. Then this whole system is a Network.

*What is Networking*

1. We know that Network is a connection of muliple devices. But What will they do by connecting?
2. By connecting they can transfer data. Suppose your laptop have a movie then you can transfer it to other devices cause they are connected. They can download something.
3. If our desktop have a movie and our laptop want that movie. then desktop will transfer that movie to mobile and mobile will transfer to laptop. Because all devices are connected to mobile thruogh same mobile hotspot.
4. for transfering data we need to follow some rules(Protocols).
5. Studying all this, how devices are comminicating, how data is getting transfered, what rules(protocols) are getting used etc. is called Networking.
